Basotho Cultural Heritage

Welcome to a journey into the heart of Basotho culture! Explore a rich heritage built on a foundation of resilience, community and profound connection to the land. Lesotho boasts of friendly people, rich cultural heritage and historic sites of pristine value and offers unique scenery to discerning tourist.

The main aim of this website is to showcase traditional clothing, music, dance, crafts and art.Raise awareness of Basotho cultural values, how they conduct themselves in society, their lifestyle and livelihood. Not only will this website promote Basotho's culture but it will encourage the youth to love their culture even more, to be proud of who they are and to know where their roots are located.
